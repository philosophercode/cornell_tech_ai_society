## What We Do

- Talks & panels with AI experts
- Hands-on workshops (in partnership with [No-Code Works](https://nocodeworks.dev))
- Discussions and debates on AI ethics & philosophy
- Creative galleries (GenAI art, media)
- Hackathons & collaborative builds

## Tech Stack

- Static HTML + [Tailwind CSS](https://tailwindcss.com) (via CDN)
- [Inter](https://fonts.google.com/specimen/Inter) typeface
- Deployed on [Vercel](https://vercel.com)
